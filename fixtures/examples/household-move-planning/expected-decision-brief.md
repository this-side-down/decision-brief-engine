# Decision Brief

## Summary

Assign one move owner today. Submit pet paperwork immediately. Confirm elevator slot and mover quote. Target a mid-month move with couch storage only if painter timing slips. Medium confidence until visa timing and painter completion are confirmed.

## Decision Context

The household must sequence move logistics under overlapping leases, building pet approval, uncertain visa timing, elevator constraints for movers, and divided ownership of utilities and address changes.

## Options Considered

- Move on the 14th with overlap rent and short-term storage for the couch
- Delay move to the 22nd to reduce overlap but risk visa timing and painter slip
- Hire movers for heavy items only and handle boxes DIY to save about $1.2k
- Use parent help for boxes and boarding for the dog during move weekend

## Recommendation

Assign a single move owner. Submit pet paperwork immediately. Confirm elevator slot and mover quote. Target a mid-month move with a storage plan for the couch only if painter timing slips.

## Risks and Constraints

### Risks

- Visa delay could block lease signing in both names and utility setup
- Painter slip could force storage costs or overlap rent extension
- Elevator hoist surcharge could blow the mover budget
- Unclear ownership could leave utilities or address changes undone
- DIY packing could slip while working full time

### Constraints

- New lease starts the 12th; old lease ends the 30th
- Budget of roughly $4–5k for movers and deposits
- Building requires pet registration before keys
- Freight elevator slot must be confirmed before mover booking
- Both partners are working full time during packing window

## Open Questions

- Will pet approval clear before the target move date?
- What is the visa outcome timeline relative to lease signing?
- Will painters finish by the 10th?
- Which elevator slot is available for booking?
- Who owns utilities, address changes, and mover coordination?

## Suggested Next Steps

- Name one owner for the move checklist today
- Submit pet records and deposit to building management
- Request elevator slot and locked mover quote with cancellation terms
- Decide sell-vs-store for the couch once storage cost is known
- Hold a 30-minute sequencing review after visa appointment

## Confidence
